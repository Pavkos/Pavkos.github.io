Aurora Pass Support

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Aurora Pass helps you check aurora conditions, plan nights outside, receive local alerts, and keep a personal sighting journal.

Contact

For support, bug reports, or account questions, email ikorkodinov@gmail.com.

Forecast Notes

Aurora forecasts are probabilistic and depend on public space-weather and weather data. Visibility can change quickly because of clouds, daylight, moonlight, local horizon, and geomagnetic activity. The app is a planning tool, not a guarantee that aurora will be visible.

Purchases

Premium purchases are handled by Apple through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Data Sources

The app uses public NOAA SWPC space-weather data, Open-Meteo cloud forecasts, and GFZ Potsdam geomagnetic data where available.
