TickBug Support

Last updated: June 5, 2026

TickBug helps you scan ticks, bites, and stings, read a calm risk summary, save bite history for people and pets, and set symptom-watch reminders.

Contact

For support, bug reports, purchase questions, or privacy requests, email ikorkodinov@gmail.com.

Medical and Veterinary Safety

TickBug provides general information only and is not a diagnosis or treatment plan. If symptoms appear, a bite worsens, a tick may have been attached for a long time, or you are worried about a person or pet, contact a doctor or veterinarian.

Purchases

Premium purchases are handled by Apple through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

Photo and Camera Access

The app requests camera or photo-library access only when you choose to take or select a photo. You can change these permissions in iOS Settings.
