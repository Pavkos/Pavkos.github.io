Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how StickerKick, also distributed as Football Card Price Scanner, handles information when you use our iOS app and related services.

Information We Collect

Card photos and scan data. When you scan a card or choose a photo, the image and related scan data may be processed to identify the card, estimate value, find comparable listings, and return results to you.

Collection and share data. If you save cards, create albums, use cloud backup, publish a shared list, or use price watch, we may store card names, teams, years, card numbers, estimated values, duplicate/trade/sale status, share codes, and related collection metadata.

Analytics and app usage. We collect app interaction events such as onboarding progress, scan attempts, scan completion, feature taps, paywall views, purchase flow events, language, storefront country, app version, device-level identifiers, and coarse location signals used by analytics providers.

Purchase information. Subscription status, product identifiers, entitlement state, renewal status, and purchase history may be processed through Apple and RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your payment card number.

Support and feedback. If you send feedback or contact us, we process the information you choose to provide.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide card scanning, valuation, collection management, album tracking, share links, price watch, subscription access, customer support, fraud prevention, diagnostics, analytics, and product improvement.

Third-Party Services

We use service providers to operate the app, including Apple, RevenueCat, Amplitude, Supabase, OpenAI, eBay, Apify, and GitHub Pages. These providers process information only as needed to provide app functionality, analytics, purchase management, cloud storage, price lookup, or public web pages.

Tracking and Advertising

We do not sell personal information and do not use your data to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain purchases and shared lists, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve reliability. You can delete local app data from your device, and you can contact us to request deletion of server-side data associated with your app identifier or share code.

Your Choices

You can deny camera or photo library access, avoid publishing shared lists, cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings, and contact us to request access or deletion where applicable.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The updated date at the top shows the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact us at ikorkod@gmail.com.
